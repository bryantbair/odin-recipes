No Bakes

Description

Stove top dessert that you won't be able to wait to cool. Chocolate peanut butter goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 cups old fashioned rolled oats (not quick oats)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Place 30 inches of waxed paper on counter
  2. Melt butter in 6 quart saucepan over medium heat
  3. Add sugar and milk and bring to a boil for 1 minute 20 seconds
  4. Remove from heat and add remaining ingredients and mix
  5. Spoon mixture onto waxed paper in approximately 1 tablespoon dollops
  6. Allow to cool and serve
